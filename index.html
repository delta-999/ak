About Me

Advocate. Leader. Servant of Justice.

Adv. Saurabh Saraswat

Advocate | Bar Association Member

Adv. Saurabh Saraswat is a distinguished advocate with years of dedicated service to the legal fraternity. Known for his integrity, hard work, and commitment to justice, he has earned the respect of colleagues and clients alike.

His practice spans civil, criminal, and constitutional law. Throughout his career, he has championed the causes of the common man and stood firm against injustice at every level.

As a candidate for the Bar Association elections, Adv. Saurabh Saraswat brings a clear vision: a stronger, unified, and more responsive Bar that truly serves its members.
